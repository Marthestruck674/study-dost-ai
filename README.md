## 📚 Features

The application provides three distinct ways to learn. You choose the method that fits your current needs.

### 1. Intuitive Explanations
The app takes any STEM concept and provides three layers of depth. You may see a high-level summary, a detailed breakdown, or a simplified analogy. This approach ensures you grasp the core principle behind the math or science rule.

### 2. Interactive Practice
Practice modules help you apply what you learned. You receive instant feedback on your answers. You see where you made a mistake and get a clear explanation on how to reach the correct answer.

### 3. Curiosity Rewards
The system tracks your study sessions. You earn points for every session you complete and every concept you master. These rewards encourage consistent learning habits.

## 🔍 How to Use the App

Open the program via the desktop shortcut. You will see a dashboard that displays your active subjects. 

Type your topic or paste a question into the text box. Choose one of the three explanation styles. The app generates your lesson instantly. If you need more detail, click the "Expand" button. 

To practice, navigate to the "Practice" tab. Select your subject and answer the provided questions. The app records your progress automatically.

## ⚙️ Troubleshooting

If the application fails to open, verify your internet connection. The app requires a connection to reach the AI servers.

If the app feels slow, close other programs that use significant system resources. 

If you see an error message during installation, verify that you have sufficient disk space. Sometimes, restarting the computer clears temporary file issues.
